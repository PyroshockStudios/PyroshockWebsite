About Pyroshock Studios

Our Story

Founded in The Netherlands, Pyroshock Studios started as a small team of passionate developers with a shared dream: to create efficient software and fun games.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make efficiency and fun the standard, and not a luxury,
To bring the market back to the consumer,
To fight against planned obselecense,
And we do this by providing private software contracts, delivering custom solutions for clients, and developing our own in-house game titles like Derby Heat. We are driven by efficiency, research, and a passion for quality, which is why we build our own tools like the Shard3D Engine.
But we also want to share our efforts, and give back to the developers, and inspire them to follow our footsteps in making a change, which is why we provide open source libraries like ShockGraph which has been extensively tested and designed to provide a wide range of compatibility, ease of access, and performance.
